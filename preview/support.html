Frequently Asked Questions
How do I start investing?
Complete your KYC verification, deposit funds into your wallet, then browse properties or commodities to find your first investment.
What is fractional ownership?
Fractional ownership allows you to own a portion of a high-value asset. You invest a fraction of the total cost and earn proportional returns.
How are returns calculated?
Returns are based on rental yields for properties and market performance for commodities. You receive your share proportional to your investment.
How do I withdraw funds?
Go to your Wallet, tap Withdraw, enter the amount and your bank details. Withdrawals are processed within 2-3 business days.
Is my investment secure?
All investments are backed by real assets. We use bank-level encryption, and all properties are legally vetted with full documentation.
