Your information is used to provide and improve our services, process investments and transactions, comply with legal and regulatory requirements, communicate important updates, and personalize your experience on the platform.

We implement industry-standard security measures including bank-level encryption (AES-256), secure data transmission (TLS 1.3), and regular security audits to protect your personal and financial information from unauthorized access.

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with authorized payment processors (Tap Payments, Burjx) and regulatory bodies as required by law. All third-party partners are contractually obligated to protect your data.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Financial records are retained in accordance with UAE regulatory requirements. You may request deletion of your data subject to legal obligations.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You may also request a copy of your data in a portable format. To exercise these rights, contact our privacy team through the app or via email.
